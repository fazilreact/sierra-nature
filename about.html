Die Naturwissenschaften was founded in 1913 by
Arnold Berliner and published by Julius
Springer Verlag.

The original subtitle Wochenschrift für die Fortschritte der Naturwissenschaften,
Medizin und der Technik (the Advances in the Natural Sciences, Medicine and
Technology) was later changed to its current The Science of Nature.The journal
is published monthly and the articles are exclusively in English

It was founded in 1913 and intended as a German-language equivalent of the
English-language. journal Nature, at a time when German was still a dominant
language of the natural sciences.

IT IS A GIFT

Gifts of nature are fruits and nuts growing on trees,
sunlight, rain, fresh flowing water, clean air, the
sound of birds singing, beautiful vistas overlooking
landscapes, the diversity of life on this planet,
colorful sunsets, rainbows, life and death, freedom,
all principles that belong to nature and her cycles.
Humans should be the stewards of nature, but sadly
we are destroying her with our actions that pollute
and damage her.We causing man-made global warming.
